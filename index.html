System Features

Signal Engine
Multi-Layer Signal Analysis
A proprietary multi-layer signal engine evaluates momentum, trend structure, volume dynamics, and market positioning simultaneously — filtering out noise before a signal ever fires.
On-Chart Delivery
Everything Paints Directly on Your Chart
No bots, no apps, no feeds. The moment a signal is valid, a Long or Short label appears on the bar. Target and stop lines draw automatically. Recovery labels tell you exactly what to do next. Green triangles confirm target hits. It all lives inside TradingView.
Trade Management
Intelligent Recovery System
When price moves against your position, the system dynamically repositions targets and stops and signals when to scale in — turning adverse moves into managed opportunities.
Risk Control
ATR-Based Risk Awareness — Know Before You Enter
Every stop is calculated from live ATR — so your risk is never fixed, it breathes with the market. The dashboard flags ATR as NORM, ELEV, or HIGH in real time. When ATR is elevated your potential loss on a stop-out is larger, even though win rate stays the same. This is your signal to size down. The system is already built on a 3-to-1 risk-reward — respect the ATR and it stays that way.
Session Awareness
Intelligent Time Filters
Built-in filters block entry during historically low-quality windows — the choppy transition periods that eat into edge. Trade when conditions are right, sit out when they're not.
